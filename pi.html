Rasberry Pi Project

Spenser Valentine

In this project, I had to create a case for a Rasberry Pi. As a class we used Fusion 360 to model what our case would be. To make the case I used digital calipers and measured the Pi itself. Then using Fusion 360 I made an outline for the case. This was hard at first but easy to learn. Fusion 360 was very intuitive and caused minimal amount of stress and anxiety. However I acidentally switched the walls making the entire case backwards, so to fix that I pulled all the walls down to the opposite plane and then flipped the entire structure. All of the pictures I had of the case were deleted from the computer reboot so here is a picture of a Rasberry Pi.
